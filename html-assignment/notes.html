<h1> - <h6>
Heading tags that define the hierarchy of content from most important (<h1>) to least important (<h6>).
<p>
Paragraph tag that defines a block of text.
<img>
Image tag used to embed images in the webpage.
<a>
Anchor tag used to create hyperlinks to other pages or locations.
<ul> and <li>
Tags used to create an unordered list. Each list item is wrapped in <li>.
