About Me

My name is Ray and Im a GD. I've been interested in art ever since I was little, expressing myself through paint and play-doh. As I grew older, I loved to discover the different ways companies creatively marketed their products, whether through social media content, packaging, posters, or on their websites.

I've always been interested in art and design since I was young (helping people was a bonus). I've always loved watching the cartoons from 1990th. As I grew older, the fondness grew and it drove me to graphic design. The program provided a bigger skillset in order to continue doing what I love.
